# Domain: Technical (Persian — FA)

## Reader Model

### Target Reader Profile
- **Primary Reader**: Iranian engineers (مهندسان), IT professionals (متخصصان فناوری اطلاعات), R&D personnel (کارکنان تحقیق و توسعه), quality control specialists (متخصصان کنترل کیفیت)
- **Secondary Reader**: Standards organization reviewers (بررسی‌کنندگان سازمان ملی استاندارد), technology ministry experts (کارشناسان وزارت فناوری), accreditation bodies
- **Tertiary Reader**: Technical procurement committees, project managers, technical translators

### Reader Expectations
1. **Terminological Precision**: Technical terms must use standard Persian equivalents; literal translations from English are unacceptable when established terms exist.
2. **Standards Compliance**: Technical documents must reference Iranian Standards (ISIRI/INSO) where applicable.
3. **Measurement Units**: SI (metric) system is mandatory; imperial units must be converted.
4. **Clarity and Conciseness**: Technical writing should avoid unnecessary adjectives; prefer direct, clear expression.
5. **Consistent Terminology**: Once a term is chosen, it must be used consistently throughout the document.

### Reader Expectation Matrix

| Expectation | Importance | Consequence if Unmet |
|------------|-----------|---------------------|
| Technical terminology accuracy | Critical | Safety risk, product failure |
| Standards reference alignment | High | Compliance failure |
| SI unit system | High | Measurement errors |
| Terminology consistency | High | Confusion, mistranslation |
| Clear sentence structure | Medium | Reduced comprehension |
| Figure/table labeling | Medium | Reference errors |

## Domain-Specific Reference

### A. Iranian Technical Standards System
Iran's technical standards system is managed by:
1. **Institute of Standards and Industrial Research of Iran (ISIRI/INSO)**: The national standards body (سازمان ملی استاندارد ایران).
2. **Adoption of international standards**: Iran frequently adopts ISO, IEC, and other international standards as national standards.
3. **Metrology**: Iran's national metrology institute provides calibration and measurement standards.

### B. Key Technical Institutions

| English | Persian | Role |
|---------|---------|------|
| ISIRI (National Standards Organization) | سازمان ملی استاندارد ایران | Standards development and enforcement |
| IT Research Institute | پژوهشگاه ارتباطات و فناوری اطلاعات | IT research |
| Innovation Fund | صندوق نوآوری و شکوفایی | Innovation funding |
| Vice Presidency for Science and Technology | معاونت علمی و فناوری ریاست جمهوری | Science policy |

### C. Translation Quality Markers
- **Acceptable**: Uses standard Persian IT/engineering terms; correctly references ISIRI/ISO standards; converts imperial units to SI; maintains terminology consistency.
- **Unacceptable**: Uses English loanwords where established Persian terms exist; mixes imperial and SI units; inconsistently translates the same technical term.
- **Review Required**: New/emerging technology terms with no established Persian equivalent; standards references requiring verification; highly specialized engineering terminology.
