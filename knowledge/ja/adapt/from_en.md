# English → Japanese Adaptation Rules

> **Target**: English to Japanese translation adaptation rules
> **Purpose**: High-quality conversion guidelines accounting for cultural background, notation conventions, and grammatical structure differences

---

## 1. Formality and Address (EN→JA)

English has a relatively simple formality system compared to Japanese. The English "you" does not distinguish formality levels, whereas Japanese requires careful selection of honorifics based on social hierarchy, relationship, and context.

### 1.1 Second Person Pronoun Conversion

| # | English (Source) | Japanese (Target) | Condition | Notes |
|---|-----------------|-------------------|-----------|-------|
| 1 | you | 貴社（きしゃ）/ 御社（おんしゃ） | Referring to a company | "Kisha" for writing, "Onsha" for speech |
| 2 | you | お客様（おきゃくさま） | Referring to a customer/client | Most common in service/retail |
| 3 | you | 様（さま） | Attached to a personal name | Surname + sama is the standard formal address |
| 4 | you | さん | Casual/equal relationship | Used in internal company communications |
| 5 | you | （omit） | When subject is clear | Japanese frequently omits the subject |
| 6 | you (plural) | 皆様（みなさま） | Multiple recipients | Most polite plural form |
| 7 | your company | 貴社 / 御社 | Business correspondence | See section 1.1 for usage distinctions |
| 8 | your team | ご担当者様 / 各位 | Group address | "Kakui" is standard for written notices |
| 9 | Dear Sir/Madam | 拝啓（はいけい） | Letter opening | Japanese uses set opening phrases |
| 10 | To whom it may concern | 関係者各位（かんけいしゃかくい） | General notice | Standard for circular notices |

## 2. Number and Date Conventions (EN→JA)

English and Japanese use different numeral systems, date formats, time notation, and measurement units. Careful conversion is required, particularly for date formats where month/day order differs.

### 2.1 Large Number Conversion

| # | English (Source) | Japanese (Target) | Condition | Notes |
|---|-----------------|-------------------|-----------|-------|
| 1 | 1,000 | 1,000 | Thousand | Same 3-digit comma notation |
| 2 | 10,000 | 1万（いちまん） | Ten thousand | English lacks "man" unit; must convert to Japanese unit |
| 3 | 100,000 | 10万（じゅうまん） | Hundred thousand | Convert to "juuman" |
| 4 | 1,000,000 (1 million) | 100万（ひゃくまん） | Million | "Hyakuman" |
| 5 | 10,000,000 (10 million) | 1,000万（いっせんまん） | Ten million | "Issenman" |
| 6 | 100,000,000 (100 million) | 1億（いちおく） | Hundred million | "Ichioku" — note: switch from 10^6 to 10^8 unit |
| 7 | 1,000,000,000 (1 billion) | 10億（じゅうおく） | Billion | "Juuoku" — English billion = 10^9 = Japanese 10 oku |
| 8 | 10,000,000,000 (10 billion) | 100億（ひゃくおく） | Ten billion | "Hyakuoku" |
| 9 | 100,000,000,000 (100 billion) | 1,000億（いっせんおく） | Hundred billion | "Issen'oku" |
| 10 | 1,000,000,000,000 (1 trillion) | 1兆（いっちょう） | Trillion | "Icchou" — English trillion = 10^12 = Japanese 1 chou |
| 11 | 1.5 million | 150万 | Decimal millions | "1.5 million" → "150万" |
| 12 | 2.3 billion | 23億 | Decimal billions | "2.3 billion" → "23億" |

| 11 | depreciation | 減価償却費（げんかしょうきゃくひ） | "Genka shoukyaku hi" |
| 12 | amortization | 償却（しょうきゃく） | "Shoukyaku" |
| 13 | dividend | 配当（はいとう） | "Haitou" |
| 14 | interest | 利息（りそく）/ 金利（きんり） | "Risoku" (interest earned) or "kinri" (interest rate) |
| 15 | merger and acquisition (M&A) | 合併・買収（M&A） | "Gappei baishuu" with M&A |

| 8 | N.B. (nota bene) | 注意（ちゅうい） | "Chuui" — expand to Japanese |

## 5. Administrative/Legal Style (EN→JA)

English administrative, legal, and official documents require conversion to Japanese bureaucratic style (法令スタイル / 公文書スタイル), which uses specific phrase patterns, formal grammar, and set expressions.

### 5.1 Legal "Shall" and Obligation

| # | English (Source) | Japanese (Target) | Condition | Notes |
|---|-----------------|-------------------|-----------|-------|
| 1 | The Contractor shall deliver... | 受注者は…を納入しなければならない。 | Obligation | "Shall" = legal obligation, not future tense |
| 2 | The Employer shall pay... | 発注者は…を支払うものとする。 | Stipulation | "Mono to suru" is standard for statutes |
| 3 | No modification shall be made... | …なくして変更を行ってはならない。 | Prohibition | "Shite wa naranai" for prohibition |
| 4 | shall not exceed | 超えてはならない | Prohibition limit | Standard legal negative obligation |
| 5 | shall be deemed | みなすものとする | Legal presumption | "Minasu mono to suru" |
| 6 | shall be entitled to | ～する権利を有する | Entitlement | "Kenri wo yuu suru" |
| 7 | shall be responsible for | ～について責任を負う | Responsibility | "Sekinin wo ou" |
| 8 | shall be governed by | ～に準拠する | Governing law | "Jyunkyo suru" |

### 5.2 "Pursuant to" and Reference Phrases

| # | English (Source) | Japanese (Target) | Condition | Notes |
|---|-----------------|-------------------|-----------|-------|
| 1 | pursuant to | に基づき / に従い | In accordance with | "Ni motozuki" or "ni shitagai" |
| 2 | in accordance with | に従い / に基づいて | Compliance | Interchangeable with "pursuant to" |
| 3 | subject to | を条件として / に従うことを条件として | Conditional | "Wo jouken to shite" |
| 4 | notwithstanding | にかかわらず / に拘わらず | Despite preceding | "Ni kakawarazu" |
| 5 | provided that | ただし / 但し | Exception/proviso | "Tadashi" — standard legal proviso |
| 6 | in the event that | ～場合には / ～ときは | Conditional | "Baai ni wa" or "toki wa" |
| 7 | for the purposes of | ～の目的上 / ～において | Scope definition | "Mokuteki jou" or "ni oite" |
| 8 | as defined in | ～において定義されるとおり | Definition reference | "Teigi sareru toori" |
| 9 | as set forth in | ～に定めるとおり / ～のとおり | Provision reference | "Sadameru toori" |
| 10 | by virtue of | により / に基づいて | By authority of | "Ni yori" or "ni motozuite" |

### 5.3 Legal Document Structure

| # | English (Source) | Japanese (Target) | Condition | Notes |
|---|-----------------|-------------------|-----------|-------|
| 1 | WHEREAS, the Company... | 会社は…を希望し、 | Contract recital | "Whereas" → plain statement in Japanese |
| 2 | NOW, THEREFORE... | よって、当事者は以下のとおり合意する。 | Agreement body | "Yotte, toujisha wa..." |
| 3 | IN WITNESS WHEREOF | 証として / 本書の証として | Execution clause | Standard execution formula |
| 4 | This Agreement is entered into... | 本契約は…により締結される。 | Opening clause | "Hon keiyaku wa...ni yori teiketsu sareru" |
| 5 | The parties hereto agree... | 当事者は、本書により次のとおり合意する。 | Agreement statement | "Toujisha wa, honsho ni yori..." |
| 6 | as amended from time to time | 随時改正される | Amendment reference | "Zuiji kaisei sareru" |
| 7 | including but not limited to | ～を含むがこれに限られない | Non-exhaustive list | Standard inclusive language |
| 8 | without limitation | 制限なく / 無制限に | Unlimited scope | "Seigen naku" |

### 5.4 Administrative Document Style

| # | English (Source) | Japanese (Target) | Condition | Notes |
|---|-----------------|-------------------|-----------|-------|
| 1 | This policy takes effect on... | 本方針は…から施行する。 | Effective date | "Hoko shin wa...kara shikou suru" |
| 2 | All employees must comply... | 全従業員は…を遵守しなければならない。 | Compliance | "Zen juugyouin wa...wo junshu shinakereba naranai" |
| 3 | Failure to comply may result in... | 遵守しなかった場合、…の対象となることがある。 | Non-compliance | "Junshu shinakatta baai, ... no taishou to naru" |
| 4 | This document supersedes... | 本書は…に優先する / 本書をもって…に代える。 | Supersession | "Honsho wo motte...ni kaeru" |
| 5 | For further information, contact... | 詳細については、…にお問い合わせください。 | Inquiry | "Shousai ni tsuite wa, ... ni otoiawase kudasai" |
| 6 | This policy applies to... | 本方針は…に適用される。 | Scope | "Hoko shin wa...ni tekiyou sareru" |
| 7 | Nothing in this policy shall... | 本方針のいかなる規定も…しない。 | Negative scope | "Hoko shin no ikanaru kitei mo...shinai" |
| 8 | The following definitions apply... | 次の定義は…に適用される。 | Definitions | "Tsugi no teigi wa...ni tekiyou sareru" |

### 5.5 Contract Style

| # | English (Source) | Japanese (Target) | Condition | Notes |
|---|-----------------|-------------------|-----------|-------|
| 1 | This Agreement is made on... | 本契約は…に締結された。 | Agreement date | Standard opening |
| 2 | Party A and Party B agree... | 甲及び乙は、次のとおり合意する。 | Agreement | "Kou oyobi Otsu wa, tsugi no toori goui suru" |
| 3 | The term of this Agreement... | 本契約の有効期間 | Term | "Hon keiyaku no yuukou kikan" |
| 4 | Either party may terminate... | いずれかの当事者は…解除することができる。 | Termination | "Izureka no toujisha wa...kaijo suru koto ga dekiru" |
| 5 | This Agreement may not be assigned... | 本契約は…譲渡してはならない。 | Assignment restriction | "Jouto shite wa naranai" |
| 6 | This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement... | 本契約は完全な合意を構成する。 | Entire agreement | "Kanzen na goui wo kousei suru" |
| 7 | Any dispute arising out of... | ～から生じる一切の紛争 | Dispute clause | "Issai no funsou" |
| 8 | This Agreement shall be governed by... | 本契約は…法に準拠する。 | Governing law | "...hou ni junkyo suru" |

## 6. Punctuation Conversion (EN→JA)

English and Japanese use different punctuation systems. English punctuation marks such as quotation marks, dashes, and ellipses have distinct Japanese equivalents. The overall spacing and typesetting conventions also differ significantly.

### 6.1 Period and Comma

| # | English (Source) | Japanese (Target) | Condition | Notes |
|---|-----------------|-------------------|-----------|-------|
| 1 | . (period) | 。（句点） | End of sentence | English period → Japanese full stop |
| 2 | , (comma) | 、（読点） | Clause separator | English comma → Japanese reading mark |
| 3 | , (Oxford comma) | （omit） | Serial list | Serial comma is NOT used in Japanese |
| 4 | , (in numbers) | 既に変換済み | Thousand separator | Keep "12,345" same format |
| 5 | . (in numbers) | 既に変換済み | Decimal point | Keep same "3.14" |
| 6 | ; (semicolon) | ； / 文分割 | Clause junction | Use Japanese semicolon or split sentence |
| 7 | : (colon) | ： | List/explanation | Use Japanese full-width colon |

### 6.2 Quotation Marks

| # | English (Source) | Japanese (Target) | Condition | Notes |
|---|-----------------|-------------------|-----------|-------|
| 1 | " " (double quotes) | 「」（かぎ括弧） | Standard quoted text | "Hello" → 「こんにちは」 |
| 2 | ' ' (single quotes) | 『』（二重かぎ括弧） | Quote within quote | Innermost quotes become 『』 |
| 3 | " " (emphasis) | 「」 | Irony/special use | Scare quotes become 「」 |
| 4 | " " (for titles) | 『』 | Book/movie titles | English quotation → Japanese double brackets |
| 5 | Italics (for titles) | 『』 / 「」 | Emphasized text | English italics for titles → Japanese brackets |
| 6 | Italics (for emphasis) | ／／ or 傍点 | Emphasized word | Japanese uses dots (傍点) rather than italics |
| 7 | ' ' (technical terms) | 「」 | First-use term | Technical terms use Japanese brackets |
| 8 | " " (dialogue) | 「」 | Speech | Dialogue quotation marks |

### 6.3 Dashes and Hyphens

| # | English (Source) | Japanese (Target) | Condition | Notes |
|---|-----------------|-------------------|-----------|-------|
| 1 | — (em dash) | ——（2本ダッシュ） | Parenthetical break | Japanese uses double dash for interruption |
| 2 | – (en dash, range) | 〜（波ダッシュ） | Range indication | "pages 10–20" → "10〜20ページ" |
| 3 | – (en dash, connection) | 〜 / – | Connection | "Tokyo–London flight" → 「東京〜ロンドン便」 |
| 4 | - (hyphen, compound) | （omit or restructure） | Compound words | "state-of-the-art" → 「最先端の」(restructure) |
| 5 | - (hyphen, prefix) | ハイフン保持可 | Prefixes | "ex-president" → 「元大統領」(omit hyphen) |
| 6 | - (hyphen, katakana) | ー（長音） | Long vowel in katakana | "player" → 「プレーヤー」(not プレイヤー) |
| 7 | - (hyphen, katakana) | ・（中黒） | Katakana word boundary | "New York" → 「ニューヨーク」(no boundary needed) |

### 6.4 Ellipsis and Other Marks

| # | English (Source) | Japanese (Target) | Condition | Notes |
|---|-----------------|-------------------|-----------|-------|
| 1 | ... (ellipsis, 3 dots) | …（三点リーダー） | Omission/pause | Same count (3 dots) in Japanese |
| 2 | . . . (spaced ellipsis) | …（三点リーダー） | Spaced dots | Condensed into Japanese ellipsis |
| 3 | …… (6 dots) | …（三点リーダー） | Trailing off | NEVER use 6 dots in Japanese |
| 4 | ' (apostrophe) | 長音 / 省略 | Contraction/possession | "don't" → Japanese has no contraction; "O'Brien" → 「オブライエン」 |
| 5 | / (slash) | ／（全角） | Alternatives | "and/or" → 「および／または」 |
| 6 | % (percent) | ％（全角） | Percentage | Use full-width percent sign |
| 7 | & (ampersand) | および / と / ＆ | Conjunction | Usually expanded to Japanese word |

### 6.5 Spacing and Typesetting Rules

| # | Rule | Description | Example |
|---|------|-------------|---------|
| 1 | No spaces between Japanese text | Japanese text does not use spaces between words | "本日は晴天です" not "本日 は 晴天 です" |
| 2 | Half-width space between Japanese and English | Insert half-width space between CJK and Latin text | "Windows システム" (with space) |
| 3 | Full-width punctuation | Use full-width punctuation marks in Japanese text | 「。」「、」「」are full-width |
| 4 | Half-width numbers and letters | Use half-width for digits and Latin letters in Japanese | 「2024年」「Windows」with half-width |
| 5 | No line-beginning punctuation | Do not place 「。」「、」 at the start of a line | Wrap previous line to include them |
| 6 | Consistent bracket nesting | Match Japanese bracket conventions |  「『 』」 for nested quotes |
| 7 | No space before % in Japanese | In Japanese text, no space before percent sign | 「50％」not「50 ％」 |
| 8 | Space after colon in English | Japanese colon usage follows different spacing | 「以下のとおり：」no space after colon |

### 6.6 Vertical vs Horizontal Writing

| # | Aspect | Vertical Writing（縦書き） | Horizontal Writing（横書き） |
|---|--------|--------------------------|---------------------------|
| 1 | Comma | 、（読点） standard | 、standard; , acceptable in tech docs |
| 2 | Period | 。（句点） standard | 。standard; . acceptable in tech docs |
| 3 | Numbers | Kanji numbers preferred | Arabic digits preferred |
| 4 | Latin text | Rotated 90 degrees | Standard orientation |
| 5 | Quotation marks | 「」standard | 「」standard |
| 6 | Parentheses | （）standard | （）standard |
| 7 | Consistency | Must be consistent throughout document | Must be consistent throughout document |

### 6.7 Japanese-specific Punctuation Notes

| # | Mark | Name | Usage Notes |
|---|------|------|-------------|
| 1 | ・ | 中黒（なかぐろ） | Word separation in katakana phrases: 「アメリカ・イギリス」 |
| 2 | 々 | 踊り字（おどりじ） | Repetition of kanji: 「人々」「段々」 |
| 3 | 〜 | 波ダッシュ（なみダッシュ） | Range: 「1〜10」「東京〜大阪」 |
| 4 | ※ | 米印（こめじるし） | Note reference mark |
| 5 | 【 】 | 角括弧（かくがっこう） | Headings and emphasis in documents |
| 6 | 〃 | 同上記号（どうじょうきごう） | Same as above (used in tables/lists) |
| 7 | 〆 | 締め記号（しめきごう） | Total/closing mark: 〆切（しめきり） |
| 8 | ヶ | 箇の略字 | Counter: 「1ヶ月」「2ヶ所」 |
