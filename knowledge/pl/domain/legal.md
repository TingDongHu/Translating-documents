# Polish Legal Domain: Reader Model + Decision Framework + Standards

---

## PART 1: READER MODEL

### 1.1 Target Reader Profile

| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Primary audience** | Polish lawyers (radcy prawni, adwokaci, notariusze), judges (sedziowie), prosecutors (prokuratorzy), legal translators, in-house counsel, court interpreters (tlumacze przysiegli) |
| **Language variant** | Jezyk prawniczy (legal professional language) and jezyk prawny (language of the law -- statutory language). Two distinct subcodes: the language of statutes (jezyk prawny) and the language of legal practice (jezyk prawniczy) |
| **Proficiency required** | C2 in general Polish + specialized knowledge of Polish legal system and Latin legal terminology used in Polish law |
| **Expected text types** | Contracts (umowy), statutes (ustawy), regulations (rozporzadzenia), court judgments (orzeczenia/wyroki), pleadings (pisma procesowe), notarial deeds (akty notarialne), official correspondence (pisma urzedowe), legal opinions (opinie prawne) |
| **Reader priorities** | Unambiguous modality (shall/may/must not), hierarchical norm structure, precise definition of terms, proper citation of legal instruments |

### 1.2 Reader Expectations

| Expectation | Manifestation |
|-------------|---------------|
| **Modal precision** | "moze" (may/permissive), "musi" / "jest obowiazany" (must/obligatory), "nie moze" (must not/prohibitory), "mozna" (it is permissible) |
| **Citation format** | Specific format: "art. 5 ust. 2 pkt 3 lit. a ustawy z dnia 15 lutego 1992 r. o podatku dochodowym..." |
| **Nominalization** | Heavy use of rzeczowniki odczasownikowe: "zawarcie umowy" (conclusion of contract), "wypowiedzenie umowy" (termination of contract) |
| **Archaic constructions** | "niniejszym" (hereby), "niniejsza umowa" (this agreement), "wszelkie" (any and all), "ponizszy" (hereinafter) |
| **Latin terms** | Retained Latin: "pacta sunt servanda", "clausula rebus sic stantibus", "conditio sine qua non" |
| **Sentence length** | Extremely long sentences with multiple subordinate clauses; characteristic of Polish legal writing |

## PART 2: DECISION FRAMEWORK

### 2.1 Polish Legal Orthography

#### Table L1: Capitalization in Legal Polish

| Context | Rule | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| Statute names | Lowercase "ustawa" except when citing formally | "ustawa z dnia..." but "Ustawa" in title |
| Court names | Full official name capitalized | "Sąd Najwyższy", "Naczelny Sąd Administracyjny", "Trybunał Konstytucyjny" |
| Parties in proceedings | Lowercase | "powod" (plaintiff), "pozwany" (defendant), "strona" (party) |
| Document titles | Sentence case | "Umowa sprzedazy nieruchomosci" |
| Legal person names | Full name including form | "Spółka z ograniczoną odpowiedzialnością" |
| Abbreviations | All caps: "DNA" (not dna) for court case number | "sygn. akt III CZP 15/22" |
| Months | Lowercase | "dnia 1 marca 2023 r." |

#### Table L2: Punctuation in Legal Polish

| Context | Rule | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| Enumeration after "w szczególności" | Colon + comma | "w szczególności: a), b), c)" |
| "tj." | Always with period | "to jest" abbreviated as "tj." |
| "tzn." | Always with period | "to znaczy" abbreviated as "tzn." |
| "r." after year | Always with period | "2023 r." |
| Quotation marks | Polish ,,..." (przecinki) | ,,Zgodnie z art. 5..." |
| Citation markers | "§" (paragraf), "art." (artykul) | "art. 5 § 2" |
| Brackets | Round brackets for citations | "(Dz. U. z 2023 r. poz. 456)" |

#### Table L3: Key Grammatical Structures in Legal Polish

| English Construction | Polish Equivalent | Register |
|---------------------|-------------------|----------|
| "X shall..." | "X jest obowiazany do..." / "X zobowiazuje sie do..." | Contractual |
| "X may..." | "X jest uprawniony do..." / "X moze..." | Permissive |
| "X shall not..." | "X nie jest uprawniony do..." / "X nie moze..." | Prohibitive |
| "Notwithstanding..." | "Niezaleznie od..." / "Bez uszczerbku dla..." | Saving clause |
| "Subject to..." | "Z zastrzezeniem..." / "Pod warunkiem..." | Conditional |
| "Without prejudice to..." | "Bez uszczerbku dla..." | Preservation clause |
| "In accordance with..." | "Zgodnie z..." / "W trybie przewidzianym w..." | Compliance |
| "For the purposes of..." | "W rozumieniu..." / "Na potrzeby..." | Interpretive scope |
| "Unless otherwise..." | "O ile..." / "Chyba ze..." / "Jesli nie..." | Exception |
| "It is hereby agreed..." | "Niniejszym ustala sie..." | Declaratory |
| "The provisions of..." | "Przepisy..." / "Postanowienia..." | Norm reference |

## PART 3: STANDARDS

### 3.1 Normative Hierarchy (Hierarchia Zródel Prawa)

```
Konstytucja RP (Constitution)
    |
Ustawy (Statutes/Acts) -- Sejm + Senat + Prezydent
    |
Rozporzadzenia (Regulations) -- issued by ministers
    |
Akty prawa miejscowego (Local law acts) -- issued by voivodes, powiats, gminas
```

- **Ratified international agreements** have primacy over statutes if ratified with prior consent (art. 91 Konstytucji)
- **EU law** (Traktaty, rozporzadzenia, dyrektywy) applies according to art. 91 ust. 3 Konstytucji
- **Rozporzadzenia z moca ustawy** (Regulations with force of statute): only during state of emergency (art. 234 Konstytucji)
