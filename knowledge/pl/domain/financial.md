# Polish Financial Domain: Reader Model + Decision Framework + Standards

---

## PART 1: READER MODEL

### 1.1 Target Reader Profile

| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Primary audience** | Polish accountants (ksiegowi, glowni ksiegowi), auditors (biegli rewidenci / audytorzy), financial analysts (analitycy finansowi), CFOs, tax advisors (doradcy podatkowi), employees of financial institutions (banki, fundusze inwestycyjne, zaklady ubezpieczen), financial translators |
| **Language variant** | Polski finansowy / rachunkowosci -- highly specialized register with extensive English borrowing for modern financial instruments but with strong Polish equivalents required by law (Ustawa o rachunkowosci). Mix of statutory accounting terminology (MSR / MSSF) and tax terminology |
| **Proficiency required** | C1+ Polish + deep understanding of Polish accounting law, the Chart of Accounts (Plan kont), and financial statement structure |
| **Expected text types** | Balance sheets (bilans), profit and loss accounts (rachunek zyskow i strat), cash flow statements (rachunek przeplywow pienieznych), notes to financial statements (informacja dodatkowa / noty obiasniajace), audit reports (sprawozdania bieglych rewidentow), annual reports (sprawozdanie zarzadu), tax returns (deklaracje podatkowe, CIT-8, PIT, VAT-7), internal financial regulations |
| **Reader priorities** | Absolute numerical accuracy (decimal comma), strict compliance with accounting act format, unambiguous distinction between tax and accounting treatment, correct differentiation of MSR/MSSF from UoR |

### 1.2 Reader Expectations

| Expectation | Manifestation |
|-------------|---------------|
| **PLN format** | "1 234 567,89 PLN" (space thousands, comma decimal, PLN after). NEVER "PLN 1,234,567.89" |
| **Accounting balance** | Left column "Aktywa" (Assets), Right column "Pasywa" (Liabilities + Equity) |
| **Polish BoA standard** | Chart of Accounts based on Rozporzadzenie MF or Zakladowy Plan Kont |
| **Double-entry terms** | "Strona Wn" (Winien = Debit) and "Strona Ma" (Ma = Credit) |
| **Specific naming** | "Koszty" vs "Wydatki" vs "Naklady" -- each has distinct meaning |
| **Period distinction** | Rok obrotowy (financial year), okres sprawozdawczy (reporting period) |
| **Tax vs accounting** | "KUP" (koszty uzyskania przychodu) vs koszty bilansowe |

## PART 2: DECISION FRAMEWORK

### 2.1 Polish Financial Orthography

#### Table F1: Capitalization in Financial Polish

| Context | Rule | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| Financial statement titles | Sentence case | "Bilans na dzień 31 grudnia 2024 r." |
| Account names | Lowercase | "srodki trwale", "naleznosci krotkoterminowe" |
| Standard references | Uppercase | "MSR 16", "MSSF 9", "Ustawa o rachunkowosci" |
| Abbreviations | Uppercase | "KPIR", "VAT", "CIT", "PIT" |
| Currency names | Lowercase in text | "zloty", "euro" |
| Month names | Lowercase | "za miesiac marzec 2024 r." |
| Fiscal year | "rok" lowercase | "rok obrotowy 2024" |
| Statutory references | "Ustawa" capitalized in title | "Ustawa z dnia 29 wrzesnia 1994 r. o rachunkowosci" |

#### Table F2: Punctuation in Financial Documents

| Context | Rule | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| Decimal separator | Always comma | "12 345,67 zl" |
| Thousands separator | Space (preferred) or period | "1 234 567" or "1.234.567" |
| Currency after amount | Space + PLN / zl / EUR | "500,00 PLN" |
| Negative numbers | Minus sign or parentheses | "-1 000,00" or "(1 000,00)" |
| Percentage | " %" with space | "23 %" |
| Table headers | Sentence case or uppercase | "Aktywa trwale" or "AKTYWA TRWALE" |
| Date in statements | "na dzien [date]" | "na dzien 31 grudnia 2024 roku" |
| Date in reports | "za okres [date range]" | "za okres od 1 stycznia do 31 grudnia 2024 r." |
| Ratios | Numeric with comma | "wskaźnik 2,5" (NOT 2.5) |

### 3.4 Polish Tax System on Financial Statements

| Concept | Accounting Treatment | Tax Treatment |
|---------|---------------------|---------------|
| Depreciation | MSR 16 / UoR useful life | Tax depreciation rates (Wykaz stawek amortyzacyjnych) |
| Amortisation goodwill | 5 years (MSR), 20 years (UoR) | 5 years |
| Impairment losses | Recognised in P&L | Not tax deductible |
| Provisions | Recognised if probable | Generally not deductible until realised |
| Revenue | Accrual basis | Generally accrual (with exceptions) |
| FX differences | P&L or OCI | Realised only (for most) |
| Lease (finance) | Asset + liability (MSSF 16) | Treated per tax law |
| Lease (operating) | Rent expense (MSSF 16 / UoR) | Rent expense deductible |
| Fines / penalties | Recognised as cost | Not deductible |
| Donations | Recognised as cost | Deductible up to 10% of income (CIT) |
| Bad debts | Impairment allowance | Deductible only after write-off or court decision |
| Inventories write-down | Impairment | Not deductible |
| Construction contracts | % of completion method | % of completion |
