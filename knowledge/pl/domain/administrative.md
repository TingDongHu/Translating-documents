# Polish Administrative Domain: Reader Model + Decision Framework + Standards

---

## PART 1: READER MODEL

### 1.1 Target Reader Profile

| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Primary audience** | Polish civil servants (urzednicy, pracownicy administracji publicznej), local government employees (pracownicy samorzadowi), applicants (strony postepowania administracyjnego), legal translators handling administrative matters, foreign entities dealing with Polish authorities |
| **Language variant** | Jezyk urzedowy (Officialese) -- a heavily formalized, frozen register of Standard Polish. Characterized by extreme nominalization, impersonal constructions, Latin-origin legal lexicon, and formulaic opening/closing phrases. Differs from legal language in being less normative and more procedural |
| **Proficiency required** | C1+ in general Polish + strong familiarity with KPA (Code of Administrative Procedure), administrative decision structure, and government organizational hierarchy |
| **Expected text types** | Decisions (decyzje administracyjne), official letters (pisma urzedowe), certificates (zaswiadczenia), permits (pozwolenia / zezwolenia), rulings (postanowienia), appeals (odwolania), applications (wnioski), regulations (zarzadzenia, uchwaly), protocols (protokoly), official forms (formularze urzedowe) |
| **Reader priorities** | Procedural correctness (elements of decyzja per KPA art. 107), hierarchy of authorities, proper forms of address (Pan/Pani always capitalized), citation of legal basis for every action, unambiguous administrative disposition |

### 1.2 Reader Expectations

| Expectation | Manifestation |
|-------------|---------------|
| **Capitalized formal address** | "Pan" / "Pani" / "Panstwo" always capitalized: "Wzywamy Pana do..." |
| **Frozen formulaic openings** | "Na podstawie art. ..." or "Dzialajac na podstawie..." |
| **Decision structure per KPA art. 107** | Oznaczenie organu, data, podstawa prawna, sentencja, uzasadnienie, pouczenie, podpis |
| **Impersonal obligation** | "Nalezy..." / "Wymaga sie..." / "Strona jest obowiazana..." |
| **Latin phrases** | Retained in administrative context: "decyzja", "postanowienie", "protokol", "sentencja" |
| **Year after date** | Always "2024 r." with the "r." (rok) abbreviation |

## PART 3: STANDARDS

### 3.1 Code of Administrative Procedure (KPA) Framework

| Element | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Full name | Ustawa z dnia 14 czerwca 1960 r. Kodeks postepowania administracyjnego |
| Publication | Dz.U. 1960 nr 30 poz. 168 (current: Dz.U. 2024 poz. 1231 ze zm.) |
| Scope | Administrative proceedings before state and local government bodies |
| Principles | Legality, objective truth, public interest, party participation, persuasion, speed, written form |

### 3.2 Instances of Administration (Dwuinstancyjnosc)

```
FIRST INSTANCE (I instancja):
    Minister / Wojewoda / Starosta / Burmistrz / Wojt
        |
    Decision issued with appeal instruction (pouczenie)
        |
        v (14 dni)
SECOND INSTANCE (II instancja):
    Minister (from Wojewoda)
    Szef Urzedu (from Director)
    Samorzadowe Kolegium Odwolawcze (from Burmistrz/Wojt)
        |
    Decision becomes ostateczna (final)
        |
        v (30 dni)
JUDICIAL REVIEW (Sadowa kontrola):
    Wojewodzki Sad Administracyjny (WSA)
        |
        v (30 dni)
    Naczelny Sad Administracyjny (NSA) - kasacja
```
